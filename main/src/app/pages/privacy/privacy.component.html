Shipping & Return Policy

SHIPPING POLICY

At Toosie Pharmacy Limited, we are committed to providing you with fast, reliable, and secure delivery of your pharmaceutical and health-related products. We ensure that your medications and health products are delivered to you safely and in a timely manner.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Prescription orders may require additional time to verify before shipping. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays may experience a delay in processing.

Shipping Costs

Domestic Orders: Shipping costs are calculated at checkout, based on the delivery address and NOT the size/weight of your order.

International Orders: We currently do not offer international shipping for medications.

Delivery Time

Domestic Shipping: Standard shipping takes approximately 1-2 days, depending on your location.

Priority Delivery: Priority delivery option is available at checkout for faster delivery, usually within 24h.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive on your 'My Orders' page, an order containing an order number (Order No), which will serve as a reference to tract your order.

Shipping Delays

While we do our best to ensure timely delivery, shipping times may be affected by factors beyond our control, including carrier delays and weather conditions. If there are any significant delays, we will notify you as soon as possible.

RETURNS

Return Policy

Your health and satisfaction are our top priority. We understand that sometimes returns are necessary, but due to the nature of pharmaceutical and health-related products, we have specific guidelines for returns and exchanges.

Eligibility for Returns

Prescription Medications: Due to health and safety regulations, prescription medications cannot be returned, refunded, or exchanged once they have been accepted.

Over-the-Counter (OTC) Products and Non-Prescription Items: We accept returns for unopened and unused OTC products, health supplements, and non-prescription items within 3 days of purchase. The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and in resalable condition.

Non-returnable Items: Certain items, including opened medications, and prescriptions, are non-returnable.

How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at toosiepharmacylimited@gmail.com with the following details:

    Order number
    Product name(s)
    Reason for return
    Photos of the product

If your return is approved, you will be provided with a return authorization and instructions on how to return the product.

Return Shipping Costs

For damaged or defective products, we will provide dispatch to collect the item, or the customer can return directly to the store after return is approved. For non-defective items, you will be responsible for returning the item. Prescription medication orders are non-returnable and cannot be refunded unless the wrong medication was dispensed and the medication is unopened.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email regarding the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be issued to your original method of payment. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to be processed.

Prescription Medication Refunds: Prescription medication refunds will only be processed in cases of error in the dispensing of medication or a defective product.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately at toosiepharmacylimited@gmail.com with your order number and photos of the damage. We will provide a full refund or replacement at no additional cost. If the product is a prescription medication, please contact our customer service team for guidance on returns and potential solutions.

Special Note on Prescription Medications

For safety reasons, prescription medications cannot be returned or exchanged unless they were dispensed incorrectly or are defective. If you believe there is an issue with your prescription order, please contact us immediately at toosiepharmacylimited@gmail.com, and our customer service team will assist you.

Secure Payment with Paystack

At Toosie Pharmacy Limited, we prioritize the security and privacy of your financial transactions. We use Paystack, one of the most trusted and secure payment gateways, to process all payments. With Paystack, you can shop with peace of mind knowing your payment details are protected.

How Paystack Keeps Your Payment Secure:

End-to-End Encryption: All payment transactions on our platform are encrypted using the latest security protocols. Paystack uses SSL encryption to protect your payment data, ensuring that it cannot be intercepted or tampered with.

PCI DSS Compliant: Paystack is fully compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS). This means that Paystack meets the highest level of security standards for processing card payments, safeguarding your sensitive data from unauthorized access.

Secure Payment Options: Paystack offers multiple payment options, including credit and debit card payments, bank transfers, and mobile money. Your payment method is securely handled through their platform, and we never store sensitive payment details.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Paystack supports Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to provide an additional layer of security. If you choose to use this feature, Paystack will require you to confirm your identity with a second verification step (such as a one-time password sent to your phone or email).

Fraud Prevention: Paystack uses advanced fraud detection algorithms to protect against suspicious or fraudulent activities. Any payment deemed suspicious will be flagged for review, helping to protect your transaction and the overall security of our platform.

How to Make a Secure Payment:

Select Your Items: Add your desired products to the cart and proceed to checkout.

Choose Paystack as Your Payment Method: When you're ready to pay, select Paystack from the available payment options.

Complete Your Payment: If paying by credit/debit card, you will be redirected to a secure Paystack page to enter your card details. For bank transfers or mobile money, follow the instructions provided by Paystack on the checkout page.

Transaction Confirmation: After completing your payment, you will receive an immediate confirmation of your transaction. You’ll also receive a payment receipt via email or SMS as proof of purchase.

Your Privacy Matters

Your privacy is important to us. We do not share or store any sensitive payment details. Paystack handles all payment processing securely, and all personal information is kept confidential in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
